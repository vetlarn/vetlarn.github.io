The Browser Company, based in New York, is a team dedicated to revolutionizing web browsing with their innovative browser, Arc, designed to be:

  • faster
  • more personal
  • creative

Arc aims to transform the way users interact with the internet by offering features like inking, highlighting, and note-taking directly on webpages, making browsing a more engaging and personalized experience
